Love Yourself: Tear

BTS released their third studio album LOVE YOURSELF: TEAR Fake love inevitably leads to loss and separation, For true love can only begin when you learn to love yourself

BTS will release LOVE YOURSELF: TEAR, their third studio album on May 18.

BTS LOVE YOURSELF series bears the message that loving oneself is the beginning of true love. Whereas the previously unveiled LOVE YOURSELF WONDER and LOVE YOURSELF HER albums expressed the heart-fluttering and tingling moments of love, LOVE YOURSELF TEAR album embodies the pain of boys faced with parting.
